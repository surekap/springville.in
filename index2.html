50 years of history

The Sureka Group is one the oldest and most respected developers of Eastern India. It has delivered some of the most iconic buildings in the heart of Kolkata such as Park Plaza and Central Plaza. It is currently developing in excess of 30 Lakh sqft. of projects across India.
